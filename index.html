LIMO Autonomous Navigation Project

This project simulates a real-world robotics training scenario using the Agilex LIMO robot. Our team modeled the test environment after the Crowne Plaza Changi Airport Hotel and applied a systems approach from SEBoK to align robot behavior with arena design. Our arena is one of eight interconnected environments, each representing a different section of Changi Airport ‚Äî including Terminal 1, the departure hall, Jewel, and more ‚Äî forming a complete simulation of the airport for the LIMO robot to navigate through.
