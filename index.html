LIMO Autonomous Navigation Project

This project simulates a real-world robotics training scenario using the Agilex LIMO robot. Our team modeled the test environment after the Crowne Plaza Changi Airport swimming pool and applied a systems approach from SEBoK to align robot behavior with arena design.
